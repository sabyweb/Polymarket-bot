## 1. The root-cause finding (why there was no progress)

Read-only Helsinki analysis overturned the prior written record (which said "dry shadow /
no fills / wallet flat"). The live bot was **placing 0 orders** (`0/5 on-book`) for hours:

- `SimpleAllocator` ranks candidates purely by `expected_daily_reward = daily_rate √ó
  q_share`, so it picked the **highest-reward-rate markets ‚Äî which are the short-dated
  daily / news / sports markets near a decisive event** (e.g. "WTI Up or Down on June 1",
  "SPY closes above $755", "US√óIran peace deal").
- Its candidate source (`/rewards/markets/current`) carries **no real resolution date**
  (the `end_date` is a `2500-12-31` reward-program sentinel) and no `game_start_time`, so
  it was **blind to time-to-resolution** and kept proposing already-/near-resolved markets.
- The **farmer's** own guards (`EXPIRY SWEEP` at <1h to `end_date_iso`, `RF_SPORTS/GAME_BLOCK`)
  then **refused all of them** ‚Üí 0 orders placed ‚Üí farming nothing.
- When such markets *did* fill (the first canary, and again during this soak), they filled
  **adversely** (price converged / news moved) ‚Üí losses. On-chain truth showed the prior
  "‚àí$85" was inflated: the Iran dump booked at limit $0.01 actually executed at $0.24
  (‚àí$8 real, not ‚àí$54) ‚Äî already addressed by FX-089.
